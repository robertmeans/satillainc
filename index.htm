Satilla, Inc. was founded on the principals of value, quality and creativity. We constantly strive to find new solutions for the challenges of the construction process. Our goal is to suit both the needs of the Owner and the design intent of the Architect. We believe that it is our responsibility to manage our client's resources as if they were our own.

Satilla is a small firm by design; we do not want to be all things to all people. We believe in managing our overhead closely and our workload carefully, this has always proven to make our projects more successful and our costs competitive. Our preference is to work with great clients and top tier architects. Over the years we have worked for the same clients and architects several times. We feel this is a testament to the relationships we build and the skill and quality that we put into our work.

Our projects range in value $15,000 to $5,000,000, we do not select projects based upon their value we select projects based upon the client and architect that are creating them.
